Places

Pikeplace Market is a historical an iconic place in the city of Seattle. Locals and visiters flock to this beautiful open market, located just a few blocks from the Puget Sound. It is a must see and experience all year round regardless if you are just in Seattle for a visit or you have made Seattle your home.

Nature

The Puget Sound is the body of water that connects to the Pacific ocean. It is scenic, iconic, and vital part of what makes Seattle....Seattle.
